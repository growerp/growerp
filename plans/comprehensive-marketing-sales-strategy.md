# Comprehensive Marketing & Sales Strategy for GrowERP

## 1. Executive Summary

The primary objective of this strategy is to drive user acquisition for GrowERP by fundamentally **dogfooding our own platform**. Rather than relying on external, disjointed marketing tech stacks, GrowERP leverages its built-in CRM, Marketing, and Outreach modules, orchestrated by our Control Center (ADK) AI agents. 

This strategy focuses on an aggressive, 4-week sprint model to land paying customers by demonstrating the power of the platform *through* the marketing they receive. By combining an omnichannel, AI-adapted content strategy with a highly personalized outbound sales motion, we showcase GrowERP as the ultimate "business-in-a-box" for SMBs.

## 2. Target Audience & Value Proposition

- **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):** SMB owners, operations managers, and founders currently running their businesses on fragile, disjointed spreadsheet systems or multiple disconnected SaaS tools.
- **Core Value Proposition:** "Run your whole company from one screen." GrowERP replaces fragmented tools with a single, AI-augmented ERP that handles everything from quote to paid invoice, including sales and marketing.
- **The "Hook":** A 2-minute assessment titled "5 Signs You've Outgrown Spreadsheets," driving prospects to our dedicated landing page (`ERP_LANDING_PAGE`).

## 3. The GrowERP Marketing & Sales Technology Stack

We are running this entire strategy on the GrowERP ecosystem, utilizing:
- **Backend (Moqui):** Handling data, REST services, campaign logic, and job scheduling.
- **Frontend (Flutter):** `growerp_sales`, `growerp_marketing`, and `growerp_outreach` applications.
- **Control Center (ADK):** Scheduled, LLM-powered (Gemini) agents that automate content creation, personalization, website chat, and triage.

## 4. Content Marketing Strategy (Omnichannel Fan-Out)

We have adopted a highly efficient **Author-Once, Publish-Everywhere** model. Content is organized around a rotating weekly theme structure: **PAIN → NEWS → PRIZE (PNP)**.

### The Content Flow
1. **Creation:** The **Content Studio Agent** (`GROWERP_CONTENT_SOCIAL`) automatically generates a single `MasterContent` piece based on the weekly PNP theme.
2. **AI Adaptation:** Using the `adapt#ContentForPlatform` loop, the AI tailors the MasterContent into three distinct formats (`POSTING`, `ARTICLE`, `MESSAGE`) suited for 6 target platforms.
3. **Distribution Rules:**
   - **LinkedIn:** Professional authority, engaging questions, no links in DMs until a reply is received.
   - **Facebook:** Conversational community posts with link previews.
   - **Twitter/X:** Punchy, hook-first threads (processed via assisted send-queue).
   - **Medium & Substack:** Evergreen, long-form SEO articles and newsletters.
   - **Email:** Direct broadcasts linking to our assessment tool.
4. **Publishing:** A recurring background job (`publish_scheduled_social_posts`) safely drains the queued posts at 15-minute intervals, respecting API limits.

## 5. Outbound Sales Strategy (The 4-Week Sprint)

Our outbound motion is designed to process high volumes of leads (e.g., 4,000 LinkedIn profiles via CSV) into qualified trials through extreme AI personalization.

### The Outbound Funnel
1. **Bulk Import:** Leads are loaded into the system via our newly implemented `import#OutreachRecipients` service, entering as `PENDING` messages.
2. **AI Personalization:** Hourly, the **Outreach Personalizer Agent** drafts hyper-personalized LinkedIn DMs and cold emails based on the prospect's title and company.
3. **Automated Sending:** The `process#CampaignAutomation` scheduler drains the queue, adhering to strict safety limits (e.g., 25 LinkedIn DMs, 100 emails daily).
4. **Conversion to Opportunity:** Prospects click the assessment link, automatically generating a scored `SalesOpportunity` (Cold/Warm/Hot) in the `growerp_sales` CRM.

## 6. Inbound Conversion & Lead Triage

When outbound efforts generate interest, our inbound mechanisms instantly capture and route the leads:
- **GrowERP SDR (Website Chat):** An always-on, RAG-grounded AI agent that greets website visitors. It answers pricing/feature questions, pushes the free trial, and requests human handoff upon detecting buying signals.
- **Lead Triage Agent:** Running every 30 minutes, this agent monitors for new `RESPONDED` messages or newly scored `SalesOpportunities`. It ranks the leads, drafts suggested follow-ups, and alerts the human team via chat.

## 7. Operations & Analytics

Continuous optimization is achieved through automated accountability:
- **Marketing Ops Digest:** A daily 9:00 AM summary generated by an ADK agent, reporting on DMs/emails sent, reply rates, new opportunities, and progress toward our 4-week customer acquisition goal.
- **A/B Testing:** Utilizing `CampaignMetrics` to compare AI-generated message templates (e.g., Subject Line A vs. Subject Line B) to continuously improve response rates.
