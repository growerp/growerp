GrowERP is a Flutter front-end for currently either Moqui or Apache OFBiz being multi company and currency. All modules can be run unchanged, natively on currently Mobile(IOS,Android) and in the browser Linux, Mac and Windows will be available soon. GrowERP is provided for the smaller businesses like hotels, restaurants, freelancers, dentists and e-commerce businesses and to larger companies wanting a mobile app for a particular ERP system function.  All apps including this one have full state management and automated tests covering at least 50% of the app and can be tried in the browser and downloaded frpm any store.  All apps are available on github: https://github.com/growerp/growerp
GrowERP is an initiative of AntWebsystems Co.Ltd.
