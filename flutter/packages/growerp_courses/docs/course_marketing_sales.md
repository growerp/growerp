# How to Start Using the Marketing, Outreach, and Sales Packages
**A Pipeline for Growth in GrowERP**

This course is designed to guide you through fully utilizing the CRM and pipeline capabilities of GrowERP. It bridges the gap between attracting a prospect (Marketing), maintaining communication (Outreach), and officially closing the deal (Sales).

## Course Overview
*   **Goal**: To build a complete, automated end-to-end sales funnel using GrowERP.
*   **Prerequisites**: Basic knowledge of the GrowERP Admin interface and having your company settings configured.
*   **Estimated Completion Time**: 2 Weeks.

---

## Module 1: The Foundation - Understanding the Pipeline
*How Marketing, Outreach, and Sales work together.*

### Lesson 1.1: The GrowERP Growth Ecosystem
*   **Objective**: Understand the distinction and flow between the three packages.
*   **Marketing (`growerp_marketing`)**: The "Top of Funnel" (ToFu) - Landing pages, lead capture forms, and broad campaigns.
*   **Outreach (`growerp_outreach`)**: The "Middle of Funnel" (MoFu) - Nurturing leads via automated emails, phone calls, and tracking engagement scores.
*   **Sales (`growerp_sales`)**: The "Bottom of Funnel" (BoFu) - Managing Sales Opportunities, Quotes, and creating standard Orders.
*   **Action Project**: Map out your company's current customer journey from initial discovery to closed deal on a piece of paper.

---

## Module 2: Capturing Interest (The Marketing Package)
*Building the entry points for your leads.*

### Lesson 2.1: Creating High-Converting Landing Pages
*   **Objective**: Set up your first Landing Page.
*   **Key Components**:
    *   Defining the primary 'Hook' and value proposition.
    *   Configuring the Call to Action (CTA).
*   **Action Project**: Create a Landing Page offering a free consultation or an assessment (linking to `growerp_courses` assessments if applicable).

### Lesson 2.2: Lead Capture and Campaign Tracking
*   **Objective**: Understand how marketing campaigns source leads into your system.
*   **Key Components**:
    *   Setting up tracking parameters for marketing campaigns.
    *   Viewing captured leads in the Marketing dashboard.

---

## Module 3: Nurturing the Relationship (The Outreach Package)
*Turning cold leads into warm prospects.*

### Lesson 3.1: Building an Outreach Sequence
*   **Objective**: Automate follow-ups for new leads.
*   **Key Components**:
    *   Creating Email Templates.
    *   Scheduling follow-up tasks (e.g., initial email, 3-day follow-up call, 7-day value email).
*   **Action Project**: Draft a 3-step outreach sequence for the leads generated from your Module 2 Landing Page.

### Lesson 3.2: Tracking Engagement and Scoring
*   **Objective**: Identify which leads are hot and ready for sales.
*   **Key Components**:
    *   Monitoring email opens and link clicks.
    *   Using engagement scores to trigger hand-offs to the Sales team.

---

## Module 4: Closing the Deal (The Sales Package)
*Turning prospects into paying customers.*

### Lesson 4.1: Managing Opportunities
*   **Objective**: Track the status of potential high-value deals.
*   **Key Components**:
    *   Converting a warm lead into a Sales Opportunity.
    *   Navigating the stages of an opportunity (Prospecting, Qualification, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won/Lost).
*   **Action Project**: Create a mock Opportunity for a lead and move it through two stages.

### Lesson 4.2: Quotes to Orders
*   **Objective**: Finalize the financials.
*   **Key Components**:
    *   Generating a standard Sales Quote from an Opportunity.
    *   Converting an approved Quote into a finalized Sales Order.
*   **Action Project**: Create a quote, add line items (products/services), and convert it into a final order.

---

## Final Project: The Complete Funnel Build-Out
**Your Capstone Project**:
1.  **Marketing**: Create a new Landing Page for a specific product or service.
2.  **Outreach**: Set up a 2-step automated outreach sequence for anyone who signs up via that Landing Page.
3.  **Sales**: Manually push a test lead through the sequence, convert them to an Opportunity, generate a Quote, and close the Order.
